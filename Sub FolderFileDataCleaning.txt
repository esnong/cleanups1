Doing so, it helps to ensure we are spending within planned expenditure and whether funding projects are kept within the expectations and requirements. To do so, we must complete a series of steps, in which previous interns have automated them to simplify workflow. The process automated involves downloading documents from our central invoice system, called SAP, in which we download monthly reports. The reports require data cleaning and thereafter, the relevant details are inputted into a central excel file.
