Each month, we conduct an overview and analysis of our finance expenditures to ensure we stay within the planned budget and that projects are funded according to expectations and requirements. To streamline this process, previous interns have automated several steps. The automated process begins by downloading reports from our central invoice system, SAP, where we retrieve monthly data. These reports then need to be cleaned, and the relevant information is entered into a central Excel file. This Excel file serves as the data source for PowerBI, where the data is visualized.
